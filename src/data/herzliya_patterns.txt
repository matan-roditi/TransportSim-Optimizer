Reichman University holds most morning classes starting at 08:30, creating heavy student traffic from residential areas to the campus.
Herzliya Pituach is a major tech hub where workers commute heavily between 07:30 and 09:30.
The Seven Stars Mall sees peak shopping traffic between 16:00 and 19:00.
The Herzliya Train Station brings in large waves of commuters from Tel Aviv every 30 minutes during morning and evening rush hours.
The Marina area attracts tourists and locals for leisure primarily in the late afternoons and evenings.
